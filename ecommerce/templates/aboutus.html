Who we are...

Omoads is an online marketplace for outdoor advertising. It is currently operating in Delhi NCR and expanding to other regions. We are providing an online platform for our users to book advertising such as gantry, unipole etc throughout the city. We do this by bringing all the owners of the billboards on our platform and showcase only authentic advertising spaces. Thus we reduce all the fuss of choosing the perfect location then trying to figure out who owns that hoarding and then contacting and negotiating a price and the amount of time for the rental by bringing all these processes together at one place and making the process blazing fast. And that too at the comfort of your home.

Today, outdoor advertising is an unorganized sector in every part of our country and we at Omoads are revolutionizing it through our platform, making it more organized, secure, authentic and fast.
