Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is Outdoor Advertising?

Outdoor advertising targets a mass market through billboards, posters, bus signs, stadium signs, subways, benches, and more. Outdoor marketing is a great tool to reinforce your brand and build loyalty.

Q2. What are the advantages of Outdoor Advertising?

1. High Conversion Rate – According to the consumer research, over two-thirds of travelers make their purchasing decisions at some location outside of their home over the course of a typical week; over half report making their purchasing decisions most frequently when they a not home.
2. More Value for your Money
3. Builds Immediate Brand Awareness: Unlike print, television or radio advertising that run multiple ads in sequence, outdoor advertising offers advertisers the opportunity to reach a captive audience with an exclusive, uncluttered message.
4. Billboards: People see them morning, noon and night – If you know your target demographic is situated in a confined geographical radius, there is no better way to get their attention than by advertising on a billboard along a major roadway.
One of the primary benefits of billboards is that they’re always on, 24 hours day, 365 days a year.

Q5. What is Traffic Light Signage?

It is most effective type of Outdoor Media. These are backlit board of size 8x4 sq. ft hanged on traffic light.

Q6. What are the advantages of Traffic Light Signage?

-Higher impressions than any type of Outdoor media for simple reason that every commuter would notice the changing traffic signals.
-It is most economical form of media relative to other forms.
-They are placed at premium locations i.e. intersection of highways, roads.

Q7. What are Unipoles?

Unipole is an OOH advertising structure mounted over a single steel pole or column. The Uni-pole is like a large billboard placed atop a high pole. Its effectiveness lies in the fact that this billboard can be seen even from long distances.
Advantages:
-Seen by vehicular traffic and pedestrians in commercial and industrial areas.
-Designed to be mass produced and posted in multiple locations to flood the market all at once.
-They provide 4 times more impressions than radio and magazines.

Q8. What is a Gantry?

“Gantry” is a structure consisting of a billboard mounted over two poles that are placed on both ends of a road, highway,etc.
Advantages:
-Visible to a larger moving traffic.
-Greater no. of impressions with respect to Unipoles.
